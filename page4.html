About Me

My name is Harrison Forman. I'm a musician who has played saxophone for four years, in addition to piano, guitar, flute, clarinet, and a few others. I started repairing instruments in April 2023 and have apprenticed under my repair tech. I aim to grow this business and continue to pursue music as both a hobby, a passion, and a side job. You can find me on YouTube @Harrison Forman Music.
